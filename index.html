ABOUT US

ELEVEN ELEVEN EVENTS is an events company that specializes in providing event balloon artistry and stage setup for a wide range of events, from baby showers, graduations, and corporate events to proposals and more.

The service of the company is to cater to your dream celebratory event through balloon artistry and setup. It caters to the individual who wants to make their dream event come through. The company ensures we make the client's visions or manifestations a reality.

"The more visions we make realities, the more dreams you can make."

ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Remo, the creative mind behind Eleven Eleven Events. With a passion for turning dreams into reality, I specialize in crafting stunning balloon artistry that adds a touch of magic to every celebration.

My journey began with a love for design and a desire to create unforgettable moments. I believe that every event should reflect the unique personality and vision of my clients, and I work closely with each one to bring their ideas to life.
